VanillaSake is a four-piece alternative rock band based out of Cincinnati.
Formed in early 2012, VanillaSake consists of:

Brian Sheridan – Vocals, Rhythm Guitar, Keys
Sean Dunn – Lead Guitar
Anthony Notaro – Bass
Will “Shaggy” Clemens – Drums

VanillaSake released their first EP, Noise on the Line, in November 2012.

... a blast of reverb-soaked, overdrive-dosed crunchy jams, dashed with hints of Jack White, My Morning Jacket, and even perhaps the almighty Zep
